Enhanced Google Search Terminal

Power up your Google searches with a terminal-like interface

About

Enhanced Google Search Terminal is a Chrome extension that provides a powerful, terminal-like interface for your Google searches. It combines the simplicity of a command-line interface with the power of Google search, allowing you to quickly and efficiently find the information you need.

Features

  • Terminal-like interface for Google searches
  • Search history with up/down arrow navigation
  • Autocomplete suggestions
  • Customizable themes (Dark, Light, Classic)
  • Reverse search functionality (Ctrl+R)

How to Use

  1. Click on the extension icon to open the search terminal
  2. Type your search query and press Enter
  3. Use up/down arrows to navigate through your search history
  4. Press Tab to autocomplete based on your search history
  5. Use Ctrl+R to perform a reverse search through your history

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Enter: Perform search
  • ↑ / Ctrl+P: Previous search in history
  • ↓ / Ctrl+N: Next search in history
  • Tab: Autocomplete
  • Ctrl+R: Reverse search mode
